However, total calorie levels < 1200 calories are not recommended and weight loss of > 2 pounds per week is also not recommended.

For a more balanced approach to a 1 pound per week weight loss, increase your activity. Decrease calorie intake by 250 calories per day and exercising to expend (use up) the other 250 calories. This approach prevents a decrease in your metabolic rate and promotes increased lean muscle mass.

Advice

If your base metabolism rate is the close to your daily calorie intake, then good job! You're on the right track.

However, for the rest of you, no worries! Here are some advices to help you out with your lifestyle!

Calorie deficit of 500 or above

Do make sure you are not skipping meals. Eat 3 balanced meals a day and please refer to a specialist should the problem persist.

Calorie deficit of 250 to 500

Try to increase intake of omega3 fats and have a balanced diet..

Calorie excess of 250 to 500

Just a little more to go! Increase frequency of exercise and strive to achieve a balanced diet.

Calorie excess of 500 or above

Cut down on excess sugar or fats, and exercise at least twice a week.
